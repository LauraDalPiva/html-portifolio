The Best Animes According to Laura

My top 3 Animes of all-time

Naruto

Follows the journey of a young ninja named Naruto Uzumaki as he strives to become the strongest ninja and earn the respect of his village while overcoming personal challenges and forming deep bonds with friends.

Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood

A fantasy anime with a complex story, compelling characters, and a deep focus on themes of alchemy and loss.

Frieren: Beyond Journey's End

A fantasy anime with a heartwarming story about a long-lived elf who reflects on her adventures.

Best Anime Movie

Howl's Moving Castle

A witch casts a terrible curse on young Sophie, turning her into an old woman. Desperate, she sets off on an adventure to find the wizard Howl, a mysterious sorcerer who might be able to help her break the spell.
